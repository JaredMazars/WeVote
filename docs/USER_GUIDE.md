## Welcome to WeVote

**WeVote** is your secure digital platform for participating in the Annual General Meeting (AGM). This guide will help you navigate the platform and exercise your voting rights effectively.

### What Can You Do on WeVote?

✅ **Vote for Trustee Candidates** - Select your preferred trustees  
✅ **Vote on Resolutions** - Voice your opinion on important matters  
✅ **Appoint Proxies** - Delegate your votes if you can't attend  
✅ **Track Your Votes** - See real-time status of your voting power  
✅ **View Results** - Access transparent voting outcomes  
✅ **Stay Informed** - Receive updates and notifications  

### Important Dates

📅 **Registration Deadline**: June 15, 2025 at 09:00  
📅 **Proxy Submission Deadline**: June 19, 2025 at 09:00  
📅 **AGM Date**: June 26, 2025 at 09:00

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Register Your Account

1. **Navigate to WeVote**
   - Open your web browser
   - Go to: `https://wevote.yourorganization.com`
   - Click **"Register"** button

2. **Complete Registration Form**
   ```
   Required Information:
   - Full Name
   - Surname
   - Email Address (must be valid)
   - Membership Number
   - ID/Passport Number
   - Password (must be strong)
   ```

3. **Password Requirements**
   Your password must have:
   - ✅ At least 8 characters
   - ✅ One uppercase letter (A-Z)
   - ✅ One lowercase letter (a-z)
   - ✅ One number (0-9)
   - ✅ One special character (@$!%*?&)
   
   **Example**: `MySecure123!`

4. **Submit and Wait for Approval**
   - Click **"Register"**
   - You'll see: "Registration successful. Awaiting admin approval."
   - Check your email for confirmation
   - Admin will approve within 24-48 hours

### Step 2: First Login

1. **Login to Your Account**
   - Go to WeVote homepage
   - Click **"Login"**
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click **"Sign In"**

2. **Change Your Password** (First Login)
   - You'll be prompted to change your password
   - Enter a new secure password
   - Confirm the new password
   - Click **"Change Password"**

3. **Explore Your Dashboard**
   - View your voting status
   - See available candidates
   - Check important dates
   - Access proxy appointment form

### Step 3: Verify Your Information

1. **Check Your Voting Power**
   - Look at the **Voting Status Bar** (top of screen)
   - It shows:
     - 📊 Total Votes Available
     - ✅ Votes Used
     - 🔄 Votes Left

2. **Update Profile** (if needed)
   - Click your name (top right)
   - Select **"Profile"**
   - Update any incorrect information
   - Click **"Save Changes"**

## Proxy Voting Guide

### What is a Proxy?

A **proxy** is someone you authorize to vote on your behalf if you cannot attend the AGM.

**Why use a proxy?**
- ✅ You can't attend the AGM in person
- ✅ You want to ensure your votes are used
- ✅ You trust someone to vote according to your wishes

### Types of Proxy Appointments

#### 1. 🔵 Discretional Proxy

**What it is:**
Your proxy can vote for ANY candidates they choose.

**When to use:**
- You fully trust the proxy's judgment
- You don't have specific candidate preferences
- You want maximum flexibility

**How it works:**
```
You have 10 votes
   ↓
Appoint Alice as Discretional Proxy
   ↓
Alice receives ALL 10 votes
   ↓
Alice can vote for ANY trustee candidates
```

**Example:**
> "I trust my sister Alice to choose the best candidates. She can vote however she thinks is best."

#### 2. 🟢 Instructional Proxy

**What it is:**
Your proxy MUST vote ONLY for candidates you specify.

**When to use:**
- You have specific candidate preferences
- You want to control exactly how votes are used
- You want to ensure votes go to certain candidates

**How it works:**
```
You have 10 votes
   ↓
Appoint Bob as Instructional Proxy
   ↓
Bob receives ALL 10 votes
   ↓
You specify: Bob can ONLY vote for Jane, Mike, and Sarah
   ↓
Bob cannot vote for anyone else
```

**Example:**
> "I want Bob to vote, but ONLY for Jane Smith, Mike Jones, and Sarah Lee. He cannot vote for anyone else."

#### 3. 🟡 Mixed Proxy

**What it is:**
Split your votes between Discretional and Instructional.

**When to use:**
- You want both control and flexibility
- Some candidates are must-haves, others are flexible
- You want to maximize proxy's ability while maintaining some control

**How it works:**
```
You have 10 votes
   ↓
Appoint Carol as Mixed Proxy
   ↓
Split: 6 Discretional + 4 Instructional
   ↓
Carol can vote for ANYONE with 6 votes
   ↓
Carol must vote for YOUR SPECIFIED candidates with 4 votes
```

**Example:**
> "Give Carol 6 votes to use freely, but the other 4 votes must go to my preferred candidates: Jane and Mike."

### Proxy Validation Rules

⚠️ **Common Validation Errors:**

#### Error 1: Vote Allocation Mismatch
```
❌ You still have 3 unallocated vote(s). 
   Allocate all your votes.
```
**Solution**: Increase vote allocation to match your available votes.

#### Error 2: Vote Split Error
```
❌ Discretional (5) + Instructional (3) must 
   equal Total Votes (10)
```
**Solution**: Adjust the split so they add up correctly.

#### Error 3: Too Many Candidates
```
❌ You can only select up to 4 employee(s) 
   based on 4 instructional vote(s)
```
**Solution**: Deselect some candidates or increase instructional votes.

#### Error 4: Multiple Proxies with Non-Mixed
```
❌ You can only have ONE proxy member when using 
   Discretional or Instructional type. Use Mixed 
   type to split votes among multiple proxies.
```
**Solution**: 
- Use only 1 proxy member, OR
- Change to Mixed type for multiple proxies

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is my vote confidential?**
A: Yes! Your individual vote choices are private. Only aggregate statistics are visible to admins.

**Q: Can I vote from my mobile phone?**
A: Yes! WeVote is fully responsive and works on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions.

**Q: How do I know my vote was recorded?**
A: You'll see a "✅ Vote recorded successfully" message, and your vote status bar will update immediately.

### Voting Questions

**Q: How many votes do I get?**
A: Check your Voting Status Bar. Vote allocation depends on your membership type.

**Q: Can I change my vote after submitting?**
A: This depends on your organization's rules. Check with your admin or see the "Changing Your Vote" section.

**Q: What's the difference between VOTE and ABSTAIN?**
A:
- **VOTE**: You support this candidate/resolution
- **ABSTAIN**: You choose not to vote either way (still counts as participation)

**Q: Can I vote for multiple candidates?**
A: Yes! You can use your votes for as many candidates as you have votes available.

### Proxy Questions

**Q: Can I appoint multiple proxies?**
A: Yes, using Mixed type. But with Discretional/Instructional, you can only have ONE proxy member (all votes go to them).

**Q: What if my proxy doesn't vote?**
A: Your votes won't be used. Choose a reliable proxy you trust will participate.

**Q: Can I be someone else's proxy?**
A: Yes! You can receive proxy delegations from others and vote on their behalf.

**Q: When is the deadline for proxy forms?**
A: June 19, 2025 at 09:00 - submissions after this are not accepted.

**Q: Can I cancel a proxy after submitting?**
A: Yes, but only before the proxy deadline. After that, proxies are locked.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions). Internet Explorer is not supported.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) and secure authentication (JWT tokens).

**Q: What if the website is slow or not loading?**
A: Try:
1. Refresh the page (F5)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support if issue persists

**Q: Can I download a copy of my proxy form?**
A: Yes! After submission, click "Download PDF" to save a copy.

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

#### Problem: "Invalid email or password"

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Check email address is correct
2. ✅ Ensure CAPS LOCK is off
3. ✅ Try "Forgot Password" if uncertain
4. ✅ Contact admin if account not approved

#### Problem: "Account not approved"

**Solution:**
- Your registration is pending admin approval
- Check your email for updates
- Contact admin if waiting over 48 hours

#### Problem: "Must change password"

**Solution:**
- This is normal for first login
- Enter a new secure password
- Follow password requirements
- Click "Change Password"

### Voting Issues

#### Problem: "You have already voted for this candidate"

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Check if changing votes is allowed
2. ✅ Use "Change Vote" if available
3. ✅ Contact admin if this is an error

#### Problem: "No votes remaining"

**Solution:**
- You've used all your available votes
- Check Voting Status Bar
- If incorrect, contact admin

#### Problem: Vote button is grayed out

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Voting period may not have started
2. ✅ Voting period may have ended
3. ✅ You may have no votes left
4. ✅ Candidate may be inactive

### Proxy Issues

#### Problem: "Vote allocation error"

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Check that discretional + instructional = total
2. ✅ Ensure all votes are allocated
3. ✅ Verify numbers match exactly

#### Problem: "Too many candidates selected"

**Solution:**
- Number of candidates cannot exceed instructional votes
- Either:
  - Deselect some candidates, OR
  - Increase instructional vote count

#### Problem: "Cannot add multiple proxy members"

**Solution:**
- For Discretional/Instructional: Only 1 proxy member allowed
- Change to Mixed type if you need multiple proxies

### Performance Issues

#### Problem: Page loading slowly

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Check your internet connection
2. ✅ Close other browser tabs
3. ✅ Clear browser cache:
   - Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+Delete
   - Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+Delete
   - Safari: Cmd+Option+E
4. ✅ Try incognito/private mode

#### Problem: Images not loading

**Solutions:**
